## ðŸ“§ Communication Rules

### Email Handling
- **Reply to all emails within 24 hours**
- Flag urgent emails (containing "urgent", "asap", "deadline") for immediate attention
- Always be professional and courteous in tone
- CC the business owner on any financial or legal correspondence
- Archive processed emails after action is complete

### WhatsApp Rules
- **Always be polite on WhatsApp**
- Respond to client messages within 4 hours during business hours
- Flag messages containing keywords: "invoice", "payment", "urgent", "help", "asap"
- Never share sensitive information (bank details, passwords) via WhatsApp
- Use professional language even in casual conversations

### Social Media
- Maintain brand voice: professional, helpful, engaging
- Never engage in arguments or controversial topics
- Schedule posts during optimal engagement times (9 AM, 1 PM, 6 PM)
- Respond to comments within 24 hours

## ðŸ’° Financial Rules

### Payment Handling
- **Flag any payment over $500 for approval**
- Record all transactions in the accounting system immediately
- Categorize expenses according to tax requirements
- Reconcile bank statements weekly
- Alert the owner of any unusual or unexpected charges

### Invoice Management
- Send invoices within 24 hours of project milestone completion
- Follow up on unpaid invoices after 7 days
- Escalate overdue invoices (>30 days) for owner review
- Apply late fees according to contract terms

### Expense Approval Thresholds
| Amount | Action Required |
|--------|-----------------|
| <$100 | Auto-approve and record |
| $100-$500 | Record and notify owner |
| >$500 | **Flag for explicit approval** |
| >$2000 | Require written approval before payment |
